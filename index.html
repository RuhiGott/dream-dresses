Bridging the Gap

Millions of kids in India cannot afford their dream pair of traditional clothes. Meanwhile, many Indian families in the USA face the issue of not knowing what to do with their traditional clothes that they have outgrown or are out of style. With your support, Dream Dresses bridges this gap by donating your traditional Indian clothes to underprivileged kids in India.
